Hey there, I'm Alejandro Espla, a senior UX/UI designer and front-end developer based in Playa Honda, Cartagena. After delving into advertising and public relations, and organizing a plethora of events, my passion for design, illustration, and technology led me to pursue a Master's in UX/UI design at IronHack.

With over 7 years in the field, I've tackled diverse challenges across various products and companies, evolving from design support to team management. Along the way, I expanded my skill set into front-end development and built a solid foundation in full-stack practices, which allows me to bridge design and technology more seamlessly, collaborating effectively with multidisciplinary teams.

Every day, I thrive on growth and learning. I'm all about teamwork and knowledge-sharing, finding inspiration in every encounter and project. My goal? To keep crafting useful, visually appealing, and intuitive solutions that enhance people's lives.

And if you're looking for me outside of working hours, chances are I'll be on a board — whether it's surfing the waves, skating the streets, or snowboarding down the slopes.
